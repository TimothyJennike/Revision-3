Syntax

selector:pseudo-class{ property;value }
This is how the syntax of a pseudo-class should look.

Pseudo-class & selectors

Pseudo-class are used to add styles to selectors and are expressed by adding a colon after a selector e.g hover, active, visited.

Hover & Active

If you hover over the Hover header,it will turn green and if you click on the Active header it will turn crimson.
